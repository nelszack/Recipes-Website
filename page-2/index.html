Quesadillas

Here are a few options to choose from.

Cowboy Quesadilla

ingredents:

black beans tortilla shells pork or chicken corn cheese sour cream step 1 cook meat step 2 spread sour cream on shell step 3 put rest of ingredents on shell step 4 put another shell on top of it step 5 cook it until warm

Vegetable Quesadilla

ingredents:
