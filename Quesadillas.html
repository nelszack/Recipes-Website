Quesadillas

Here are a few options to choose from.

Cowboy Quesadilla

ingredents:

  • black beans
  • tortilla shells
  • pork or chicken
  • corn
  • cheese
  • sour cream

steps:

  1. cook meat
  2. spread sour cream on shell
  3. put rest of ingredents on shell
  4. put another shell on top of it
  5. cook it until warm

Vegetable Quesadilla

ingredents:

  • tortilla shells
  • corn
  • cheese
  • sour cream

steps:

  2. spread sour cream on shell
